Sourdough Discard Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Time

    • Prep Time
      1. 20 Minutes
    • Cook Time
      1. 14 Minutes
    • Chilling Time
      1. 1 Hour
    • Total Time
      1. About 1 Hour 34 Minutes
    • Course
      1. Dessert
    • Cuisine
      1. American
    • Servings
      1. About 24

  2. Ingredients

    • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 3/4 teaspoon salt
    • 12 tablespoons (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted + cooled slightly
    • 3/4 cup packed brouwn sugar
    • 1/2 cup white sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup sourdough starter discard
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
    • 1 (12 ounce) bag semi sweet (hershey) chocolate chips

  3. Instructions

    1. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in medium bowl, set aside
    2. to bowl of a stand mixer, with padde attatchment, add melted butter and both sugars, mixed until well combined
    3. Add egg and mix until combined and fluffy
    4. Add the sour dough discard and vinalla and mix
    5. Add half of the dry ingredients and mix on low speed just until flour is moistened
    6. Scrape down the sides of the bowl and add the remaining half
    7. Mix until just incorporated - be careful not to overmix!
    9. Stir in the chocolate chips
    10. At this point you can chill the dough or bake immediately
    11. If chilling: Cover the mixing bowl and refigerate the dough for at least an hour, up to 24 hours
    12. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    13. Form cookie dough balls into golfball siz balls and place on parchment lined baking sheets
    14. Bake for 12-14, until cookies are set and are just beginning to get some color on the tops.
    15. Cool for a couple of minues on the baking sheet before removing to a wire rack to cool comepletly (or, however long u can wait!)
      • Leftovers kept in an airtight container for up to 5 days

  4. ENJOY!
