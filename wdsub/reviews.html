Avenged Sevenfold Album Reviews

My favorite band is the classic metal band Avenged Sevenfold, often abbriviated with a7x. This band has probably the best skilled classic meatal players of all time, in my opinion.

Diamonds In The Rough

The reason i choose Diamonds In The Rough as my number one favorite album, is because it has probably some of the best electric guitar solos out there. They are not as complicated as some other solos on other albums, but they are amazing. They also have a cover of "Walk" by Pantera, on their album Vulger Display of Power, on this album too and I think that is excedingly cool.

Waking The Fallen

This album Waking The Fallen is my second favorite album due to its simple complexity and interesting ways of playing. Such as Remenissions, Desecrate Through Reveerence, Eternal Rest, and Radiant Eclipse.

Life Is But A Dream

This album Life Is But A Dream has got to be one of Avenged Sevenfolds weirdest albums, but I absolutely love it. The one song that even got me started loving the album is the song "Nobody", the guitar solo on that song is insane and is what made me listen to the rest of this album.

Hail To The King

This album, Hail To The King, now this album was tough to put ranked at numbr 4 of my favorite albums, this is because it is extremely good like the Waking The Fallen album. BUT, it is here simply because the Waking The Fallen album had those songs I listed. This is kind of like that album, but the main difference is difficulty, and that can be told by even starting to listen to it, it guitar riffs are more complex, and the guitar solos are way more difficult.

Nightmare

As much as I love the ablum Nightmare, I have to put it at number 5. I have almost every song ecxept one on my playlist, but it is here because compared to the other albums, I only truly love 3 songs. Welcome To The Family, Save Me, and So Far Away.
